Pronunciation Tips

1. The "hl" sound is unique to Zulu and is pronounced by pressing your tongue to the roof of your mouth and blowing air out the sides.
2. Zulu is a tonal language, so listen carefully to the intonation, as pitch can change the meaning of words.
