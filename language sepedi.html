Pronunciation Tips

1. The "tl" sound is unique to Sepedi; practice it by placing your tongue against the roof of your mouth.
2. Pay attention to intonation; Sepedi is a tonal language, meaning the pitch can change the meaning of words.

Cultural Note

Sepedi is rich in history and culture, reflecting the heritage of the Northern Sotho people. Understanding the language deepens your appreciation of their customs and traditions.
